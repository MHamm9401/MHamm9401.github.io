Piston Fluid-Structure Interaction Simulation

In this project, I simulated compression of an ideal gas in a 2D piston in Python without the use of dedicated CFD libraries.

Motivation

As a biomedical engineer, I am interested in modeling the cardiovascular system. In particular, I am interested in modeling cardiac flows, which is often accomplished through fluid-structure interaction simulations with moving walls. I wanted to maintain the concept of a moving wall while simplifying the problem to a practical level. Therefore, I chose to model a piston compressing an ideal gas.
