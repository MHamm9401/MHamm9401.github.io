Piston Fluid-Structure Interaction Simulation

In this project, I simulated compression of an ideal gas in a 2D piston in Python without the use of dedicated CFD libraries. This involved coding a finite volume method (FVM) solver with Lax-Friedrichs artificial viscosity to solve the Euler equations for compressible inviscid flows and creating a custom boundary condition to simulate a moving wall.

Artistic Display of Research: Evolution of Turbulence in Stenotic Blood Flow

My entry to the Purdue Biomedical Engineering Department's annual Artistic Display of Research competition consisted of a high-resolution computational fluid dynamics simulation of an idealized stenotic blood vessel and fantastical kaleidoscope-like patterns created with creative post-processing.
